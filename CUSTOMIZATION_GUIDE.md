## 🎯 Tips for Good Keywords

✅ **DO:**
- Use lowercase (the script converts everything to lowercase)
- Use specific terms (e.g., "next.js" not just "framework")
- Add tools/platforms you actually use
- Include problem words ("error", "debug", "help")

❌ **DON'T:**
- Add too many generic words ("the", "and", etc.)
- Duplicate keywords
- Use keywords you don't care about
